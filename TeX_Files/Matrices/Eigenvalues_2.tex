\bexo
Let $\xi\neq 1$. What are the eigenvalues $p$ and the associated eigenvectors of 
\begin{equation}
\begin{bmatrix}
-2\omega_0\xi  & -\omega_0^2\\
 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
\eexo
\solution{
\begin{equation}
p^\pm=\omega_0\left(
-\xi\pm\sqrt{\xi^2-1}
\right).
\end{equation}
One eigenvector is
\begin{equation}
 \tb{X}^\pm=\left\{
\begin{array}{c}
p^\pm\\
1
\end{array}
\right\}
\end{equation}}
