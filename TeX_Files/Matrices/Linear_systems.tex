\bexo
What is the solution of 
\begin{equation*}
\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & 0 \\
0 & 5
\end{array}
\right]
\tb{X}=
\left\{
\begin{array}{c}
5 \\
3
\end{array}
\right\}
\end{equation*}
\eexo\solution{
\begin{equation*}
\tb{X}=
\left\{
\begin{array}{c}
\dfrac{5}{3} \\
\dfrac{3}{5} 
\end{array}
\right\}
\end{equation*}
}
