\bexo
What is the dimension of the nullspace\footnote{the set of vector $\tb{x}$ so that $[\tb{M}]\tb{x}=\tb{0}$} of matrix  
\begin{equation}
[\tb{M}]=\begin{bmatrix}
1 & -1 & 0\\
-1 & 2 & -1\\
0 & -1 & 1	
\end{bmatrix}.
\end{equation}
\eexo
\solution{ 
Let $\tb{x}=\{x,y,z\}$ be one element of the nullspace with $x$, $y$ and $z$ in $/mb{R}$. The relation between components are
\begin{align}
	x-y&=0,\\
	-x+2y-z&=0,\\
	-y+z&&=0.
\end{align}
Hence
\begin{equation}
	x=y=z	
\end{equation}
The nullspace is then of dimension 1.
}
